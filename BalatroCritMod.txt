BalaCrit:

Gives the ability to crit with cards (Jokers and Consumables maybe later). Crits double the amount of Chips and Mult in the current score.

Joker Ideas:

1. Cheap Trick : this Joker gains 15 Chips everytime a card crits. (common)
2. x : this Joker gains +1 Mult everytime a card crits. (uncommon)
//3. x : this Joker gains x0,25 Mult everytime a card crits. (rare)
4. Lucky Draw : this Joker has a 1/4 chance to give 2$ everytime a card crits. (uncommon)
5. Less is More : this Joker gives 1% crit chance for every card under standard deck size (uncommon)
6. x : This Joker gives 2% crit-chance every 10 cards played. (legendary)
7. True Strike : Allows all Cards (Jokers and consumables) to crit (chance halved). (Rare)
8. Surge : The next 8 played Cards have +100% crit chance. (uncommon)
9. Critical Patience: At the end of each hand, for every card that didn't crit the chance to gain a bonus increases (Resets after Crit). (Rare)
	x% chance to give x Chips.
	x% chance to give x Mult.
	x% chance to give x$.
10. Momentum : Gain 2% Crit Chance for every card played this hand. (Common)
11. Tight Grip : Gain 4% Crit Chance for every card held in hand. (Uncommon)
12. Final Gambit: Gives +35% critical Chance on final hand. (rare)
13. x : reduce critical chance by x%, gives 4$ at the end of round. (common)
14. x : reduce critical chance by x%, has a 1/4 chance to be destroyed if 3 or more cards crit. (common)
15. x : Either gives -50% or +200% crit chance. (rare) (only obtainable if 14. is destroyed)


Consumable Ideas:

1. The Spear : increases crit chance by 5%. (tarot card)
2. Breach : Increases the crit chance cap by 15 %. (spectral card)

Voucher Ideas:

1. Hone : Increases critical chance by 15%.
2. Uncage : +2 Consumable Slots. Raises max critical chance by 100%.


Deck Idea:

1. x : starts with 25% crit chance and one copy of "Breach" 


Blind Ideas:

1. x : critical strikes have a 1/2 chance to backfire
